Milat Foundation for Industry, Research, and Development

Vision

To provide a common platform for Professionals across industries to share, network, co-ordinate and co-operate; help them achieve excellence in their respective fields.

Mission

We aim to achieve our vision by creating a conducive platform that - Motivates professionals to embark on an Entrepreneurial journey, Provides Career Guidance to budding professionals.

Volunteering

Promotes the culture of Up skilling to stay relevant in the industry, Facilitates volunteering of time to equip needy school children with skills to become better individuals
